Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Cochin - Munnar

Welcome to Kerala. Arrive at Cochin airport and transfer to Munnar for your stay. Rest of day is at leisure. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 2: Munnar – Local Sightseeing

Today proceed for full day Munnar Sightseeing, visit Mattuppetty dam, Echo Point, Gundala Lake, Photo Point, Cinema Shooting Point, Jungle Honey Bee Nest, Pothamedu Sun. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 3: Munnar - Thekkady

Early morning after breakfast check-out and proceed to Thekkady. Arrive in Thekkady and visit Periyar wild life sanctuary where you can see wild elephant, boar, deer, the great India tiger and more, mullaperiyar dam and spice plantations tour where one can shop exotic spices. Overnight stay in Thekkady.

Day 4: Thekkady - Alleppey

Today after breakfast transfer to Alleppey. Visit Alleppey beach, sunset and enjoy shopping. Overnight stay in Alleppey.

Day 5: Alleppey - Kovalam

Check out and proceed to Kovalam. Arrive and check in at hotel. Rest of day is at leisure. Overnight stay in Kovalam.

Day 6: Kovalam

Laze on the golden sands and walk on it, to marvel at lush greenery all around. Visitors can experience the tremendous joy of surfing, swimming, skiing and kayaking in the vast azure water of Arabian Sea which touches the golden sands of Kovalam. Overnight stay in Kovalam.

Day 7: Kovalam - Cochin

After early breakfast proceed to Cochin. Arrive in cochin & visit navy museum, St. Francis Church, Chinese fishing net, Mattancherry Palace, Jewish Synegouge, Marine walk way, boating, Tripunitura hill palace and enjoy shopping. Overnight stay in Cochin.

Day 8: Cochin - Departure

After breakfast it’s time to check out. Transfer to the airport for your onward flight or back home.
