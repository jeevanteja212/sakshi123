Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Mahabaleshwar – Local sightseeing

Welcome in Mahabaleshwar. Proceed to Hotel on your own. Arrive and check in at hotel. Surrounded by lush greenery, Venna Lake, is a major tourist attraction in Mahabaleshwar. Return to hotel & enjoy dinner. Overnight stay in Mahabaleshwar.

Day 2: Mahabaleshwar – Local sightseeing

Take in the essence of Mahabaleshwar by visiting the Mapro Garden, a place where strawberries grow in abundance. Later in the afternoon, you can make your way to Pratapgarh Fort and lose yourself in the stories of heroism and fierce battles. In the evening, watch the sunset from the famous Elephant's Head Point, which as the name suggests, is in the shape of an Elephant's head. Dinner & overnight stay in Mahabaleshwar.

Day 3: Mahabaleshwar – Departure

After breakfast it’s time to check out. Transfer to next destination or hometown by your own.
